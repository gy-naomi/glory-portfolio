About Me

I am a data analyst passionate about turning business questions into structured analysis and clear visual stories. I enjoy working with complex datasets and translating them into insights that support real decisions.

With experience across operational and business environments, I’ve developed dashboards, automated reporting processes, and strengthened data validation workflows to improve accuracy and efficiency. I bring both analytical depth and business awareness — ensuring that insights are not only technically sound but also practically useful.

At my core, I believe good data work should be clear, purposeful, and impactful.

I am also building “Data Diva,” a platform focused on sharing practical Excel and data analysis insights.
